Reseting yur device

Reseting your device is an easy way to clear all your current device and turn it to a factory new state. Before you complete a reset please read these instructions through and ensure the following:

  • You have retrieved any files or photos you wish to keep.
  • Rembered all your passwords and accounts you will need access to.
  • Understand that all apps of the device will be wiped.
  • Have a backup if at all possible.
  • YOU DONT KNOW WHAT YOUR DOING, CONSULT A PROFFESIONAL!

Once you are sure you wish to procced do the following:

  1. Turn off device.
  2. Press and hold the power button while also holding the volume down button.
  3. once in the bios select reset device.
  4. Follow on screen instructions.

And that it, now you can reset your device and use it like new.
