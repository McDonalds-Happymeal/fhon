Setting up you new phone

We know how exiting it is to get a new phone and to get it up and running as soon as possible. Follow these instructions and well ahve your phone running as qucikly as possible.

  1. Remove your phone and additional equipment from packaging.
  2. Remove back panel from your device.
  3. Place in you SIM card and MicroSD (if availible) into the alloted slots in the back.
  4. Take battery from protective wrap and place inside the device.
  5. recover with back panel.
  6. Charge phone for at least 2 hours before use.
  7. Turn your phone on and follow onscreen instructions.

Thats all there is to it; enjoy your new device!
