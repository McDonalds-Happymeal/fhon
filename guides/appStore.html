The app store

The app store is your one stop shop for all the games, tools and utilities you will ever need. Through the appstore you download application that you are then able to use on your phone.

when you first get your phone the app store will be on your homescreen under google play store. You can also access the store through your app drawer by swiping up on the home screen.

Once on the app store you can choose any app or game you want and it will be downloaded to your device. you can also buy products through a google account.
